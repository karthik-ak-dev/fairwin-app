Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

TL;DR: We don't require personal info. Your wallet address is your identity.

1. What We Collect

  • Wallet Address: Public address when you connect
  • Transactions: Recorded on public Polygon blockchain
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data

2. What We Don't Collect

No name, email, phone, address, ID, or bank info.

3. How We Use Data

  • Process entries and payouts
  • Show your history and stats
  • Improve the platform
  • Prevent fraud

4. Blockchain Transparency

All transactions are on the public blockchain. Your wallet and history are publicly viewable. This enables verification.

5. Third Parties

We use RainbowKit, Polygon, and Chainlink. They have their own policies.

6. Your Rights

Disconnect your wallet anytime. Use without an account.
